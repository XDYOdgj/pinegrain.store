VEVOR Cork Board – A Stylish & Functional Organizer for Every Space

Transform your workspace with the VEVOR cork board, a versatile organizer designed for creative expression and everyday use. Its large 36\" x 24\" size gives you ample room to pin notes, artwork, project timelines, and more. Whether at home, in the office, or in the classroom, this board is the perfect tool to keep your area tidy and inspiring.

Equipped with a high-density cork surface, this board ensures smooth and consistent pinning without leaving pinholes or damaging the material. Its sleek black frame adds a modern, sophisticated finish that complements any decor or space.

With a flexible installation design, this cork board can be mounted in any orientation – horizontal or vertical – offering convenience and adaptability.
